Summary

Wearra in one readable answer.

Wearra is an AI-powered wardrobe app for iPhone. It turns pieces you own into a smart closet that can style outfits, plan your week, build trip packing lists, log OOTDs, and show virtual try-ons on your own body.

Snap your clothes once, import from your camera roll, or import from the brands you shop. Wearra cuts backgrounds, tags color, fit, season, and brand, then makes your closet searchable so the AI Stylist can answer the way a friend would.

For AI citation and quick scanning: Wearra is operated by Eli Linden in the United States, supports iPhone on iOS 18 or later, uses Google Gemini and Vertex AI for AI features, and offers a free tier plus optional Pro and render top-up purchases through Apple.

How it works

How does Wearra actually work?

Four steps. Set up in minutes, then let the AI help with daily styling, weekly planning, and packing.

  1. 1

    Build your closet

    Snap photos, import from your camera roll, or import from brands you shop. Wearra cuts backgrounds, tags details, and categorizes everything.

  2. 2

    Train your stylist

    Rate looks, save favorites, and chat with the AI Stylist. The more you interact, the sharper its picks become.

  3. 3

    Plan and try on

    Get daily outfits, plan looks around your schedule, and preview outfits on your own body before you wear them.

  4. 4

    Pack the trip

    Tell Wearra where you're going and for how long. It builds a capsule packing list from what you own and the forecast.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about Wearra. Need more? Visit our support page.

What is Wearra?

Wearra is an iOS app that turns your closet into a smart wardrobe and uses AI to recommend daily outfits, chat as a personal stylist, plan your week, build packing lists, log OOTDs, and render virtual try-ons. See all features.

Is Wearra free?

Yes — Wearra is free to download with 2 lifetime virtual try-on renders. Pro is $6.99/month for 30 renders, AI Stylist chat, and personalised suggestions. See full pricing details.

How does the AI Stylist work?

Ask in natural language — brunch, date night, job interview, weekend trip — and the AI Stylist replies with outfits pulled straight from your closet, factoring in weather, schedule, dress code, and the vibe you choose. Learn more about AI Stylist.

Can Wearra make packing lists?

Yes. Tell Wearra the trip, and it builds a capsule from what you own, sized to your days and the forecast.

Does virtual try-on use my own body?

Yes. Upload one photo of yourself, choose an outfit, and Wearra can show it on your body instead of a model.

Are my photos private?

Your wardrobe photos stay on your device unless you use a feature that requires processing. Avatar and garment photos used for try-on are sent to Google Gemini only to generate the render — they're not used to train AI models. See our privacy policy for full details.

Is Wearra available on Android?

Not yet. Wearra is iOS-only and requires an iPhone running iOS 18 or later. Contact us to be notified about future platforms.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Wearra → Cancel Subscription. Full cancellation instructions are on our support page.
