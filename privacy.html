Privacy Policy

AI-Outfits · Effective April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information AI-Outfits collects when you use the app, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We try to say this in plain English — if anything is unclear, email us at batchcompressvideos@gmail.com.

AI-Outfits is operated by Eli Linden, a sole developer based in California, USA ("we," "us," or "AI-Outfits").

What we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Account info. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name.
  • Photos you upload. Wardrobe item photos, avatar photos, and garment photos you submit for virtual try-on rendering.
  • Wardrobe details. Outfit notes, purchase prices, brand names, and style preferences you enter.
  • Feedback. Bad-render reports or other feedback you send us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information. Device model and iOS version.
  • Crash reports. Diagnostic data when the app crashes, collected via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Usage events. Which features you use and when, via Firebase Analytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and fix problems.
  • DeviceCheck token. An opaque, per-device token provided by Apple's DeviceCheck. This is not your Apple ID, not the IDFA, and cannot be used to identify you across other apps. We use it only to store two bits of information per device to prevent abuse of free credits (see "How we use it" below).

Information stored only on your device

Your full wardrobe, outfit history, and packing/trip data are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. They are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose a feature that requires it (for example, uploading a specific garment for try-on rendering).

Information uploaded to Firebase

The following is stored on Google Firebase under our account:

  • User profile (email, display name, subscription status)
  • Credit grant records and transaction history
  • Photos submitted for try-on rendering (temporarily, while being processed)
  • Bad-render reports and attached images
  • Debug logs for troubleshooting

How we use it

  • To run the core try-on feature: we send your avatar and garment photos to third-party AI providers (listed below) so they can generate a rendered image of you wearing the item.
  • To power AI outfit suggestions and chat: your prompts and wardrobe context may be sent to Google Gemini.
  • To manage your subscription: Apple handles billing; we receive subscription status so we can unlock Pro features.
  • To prevent abuse: we use Apple's DeviceCheck to flag devices that have already claimed free starter credits, so the same iPhone cannot create unlimited free accounts.
  • To fix bugs and improve the app: crash reports and usage analytics.
  • To respond to you: when you email us for support.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not show ads. We do not use your photos to train AI models on our end, and we contractually require our try-on providers to process them only for the purpose of returning a render to you. You should still review each provider's policy (below) to understand their own practices.

Your rights

Everyone

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: delete your account and associated cloud data from within the app, or email us and we'll do it for you.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you live in California, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined by the CCPA.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email batchcompressvideos@gmail.com. We will verify your request using the email address associated with your account.

EU / UK residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

  • Access your data and receive a copy in a portable format.
  • Have your data corrected or erased.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our legal bases for processing are: contract (to provide the try-on service you requested), legitimate interest (abuse prevention, debugging, improving the app), and consent (optional analytics where required). When your data is transferred outside the EEA/UK — for example, to our servers or to US-based AI providers — we rely on standard contractual clauses or the providers' own transfer mechanisms.

Data retention

  • Try-on photos. Stored briefly during processing and deleted from our Firebase Storage after the render completes or within 30 days, whichever is sooner. Retention on the third-party provider's side is governed by their policy.
  • Account data. Kept for as long as your account is active.
  • Account deletion. When you request account deletion, we delete your Firebase data within 30 days. Some information may remain in backups for a short additional period before being overwritten.
  • On-device data. Your wardrobe, outfit history, and trip data stay on your iPhone and are removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Crash/analytics logs. Retained according to Firebase's defaults (typically up to 14 months for analytics; shorter for crash logs).

Children's privacy

AI-Outfits is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has given us information, email batchcompressvideos@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Security

Data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Data at rest in Firebase is encrypted by Google. Access to our backend is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, show a notice inside the app the next time you open it. Your continued use of AI-Outfits after the change means you accept the updated policy.
